2. PURPOSE OF THE APPLICATION

The App is designed exclusively for:

  • Measuring visual acuity (distance and near)
  • Screening and preliminary assessment
  • Use in clinical practice, research, field-based community screening, and training

The App uses an adaptive 1-up/1-down staircase algorithm, optotypes based on logMAR principles, and device-calibrated sizing based on rigorous technical specifications.

The App helps users generate vision screening results, but it does not detect diseases, does not diagnose conditions, and does not provide medical treatment.
