Dyslexia Screening Architecture

A multi-modal, automated system combining traditional reading tests with AI handwriting analysis.

⏱️ Phase 1: Assessment Total: 30-35 minutes

Input 1: Digital/Oral Tasks 25-30 min

  • RAN: Letters + Digits
  • Phonological Awareness: Rhyme, Syllable, Phoneme
  • Fluency: Oral Reading Fluency & Nonsense Word Fluency
  • Comprehension check

Input 2: Handwriting 3-5 min

Student writes a standardized passage on ruled paper.

⚙️ Phase 2: AI & Automated Analysis

Phonological Awareness
15%
  • Rhyme Detection (0-80 pts)
  • Syllable Segmentation (0-60 pts)
  • Phoneme Blending (0-40 pts)
Raw Total: 0-180 (Normalized to 100)
Subscore: (0-100) × 0.15 = 0-15 pts
Rapid Automatized Naming
25%
  • Letter naming speed
  • Digit naming speed
  • Benchmark: 50 items/min
  • <40 = high risk
Raw: 100 × (Actual/50)
Subscore: (0-100) × 0.25 = 0-25 pts
Decoding (Nonsense Words)
15%
  • Correct words in 1 minute
  • Accuracy %
  • Benchmark: 20-25 wpm @ 90%
  • <10 wpm = significant deficit
Raw: (Actual/Benchmark) × 100
Subscore: (0-100) × 0.15 = 0-15 pts
Reading Fluency (ORF)
15%
  • Words Correct Per Minute (WCPM)
  • Accuracy penalty applied
  • Benchmarks: Gr2(50), Gr3(70), Gr4(90)
Raw: (Actual/Benchmark) × 100
Subscore: (0-100) × 0.15 = 0-15 pts
Handwriting Analysis
20%
  • Spacing consistency (inter-word variance %)
  • Size consistency (height variance %)
  • Baseline deviation
  • Slant angle consistency
  • Legibility score (0-100)
Subscore: (0-100) × 0.20 = 0-20 pts

📊 Phase 3: Risk Assessment & Reporting

FINAL SCORE = (HW × 0.20) + (PA × 0.15) + (RAN × 0.25) + (Dec × 0.15) + (Flu × 0.15)
✓ LOW RISK
Score: 0 - 30
  • • Continue regular instruction
  • • Reassess annually
  • • Monitor development
⚠️ MODERATE RISK
Score: 30 - 70
  • • Recommend Tier 2 intervention
  • • Specialist evaluation in 4-6 wks
  • • Reassess every 6-8 weeks
🚩 HIGH RISK
Score: 70 - 100
  • • Strong evidence of dyslexia
  • • URGENT specialist referral
  • • Begin Tier 3 intervention
  • • Consider IEP/504 plan

⚖️ Why These Weight Percentages?

Domain Weight Rationale Research Support
RAN 25% Single best predictor of reading disability 25-30% of reading variance explained
Handwriting 20% Motor/visual-spatial processing. *Serves dual purpose: detects dyslexia (5-10%) and dysgraphia/motor issues (10%). 5-10% of reading variance; 20% for catching motor issues
Phonological Awareness 15% Core phonological processing component 15-20% of reading variance
Decoding (NWF) 15% Direct measure of phonetic processing 15-20% of reading variance
Reading Fluency (ORF) 15% Actual functional reading outcome 10-15% of reading variance
